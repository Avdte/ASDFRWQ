How to Use QuickShrink

  1. Step 1: Click on the upload area or drag & drop your image (JPG, PNG, WebP).
  2. Step 2: Adjust the compression quality using the slider. 80% is recommended for good balance.
  3. Step 3: Click “Compress Now” to process the image.
  4. Step 4: Scroll down to see a preview of the original and compressed images.
  5. Step 5: Click “Download Compressed Image” to save it to your device.

About This Website

QuickShrink is a free, simple, and fast image compression tool. You don’t need to sign up or install anything. It works directly in your browser and supports all popular image formats. Whether you want to upload lighter images to your website, share smaller files on social media, or save space on your phone or PC — QuickShrink makes it easy. Safe, user-friendly, and always free.
